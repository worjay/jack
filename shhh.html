When I woke up after my first morning nap I heard a strange noise.

I knew it wasn't me because I told myself not to make any sounds,

and I always listen to myself and didn't make a sound, not even ONE sound.

It sounded like pawsteps only squeeky/squishy.

It may have been 2 dead rain soaked ploppers

tied under something or someone's feet, one for each foot, unless they hopped.

Maybe 2 more for the front feet which they didn't

seem to be using just now. Just 2 paw sounds alternating back and forth.

There was also a slow leak in the head which sounded like the wind,

but only from the direction of the head, no other wind....very strange.

It sounded painful...maybe the ploppers were screaming, I don't know.

I stayed very still and counted to 2, ten times.

The ploppers took the sound away and I had another nap.
