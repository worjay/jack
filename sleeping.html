My tail, my tail, my tail!!! somebody bit my tail, my tail, my tail somebody bit my tail!!!

I was fast asleep😴, dreaming of fish🐡, big delicious, crunchy fish, smelly fish, slimy fish, orange and white🐠, grey and black, ugly and even uglier🐟.

I was completely content when all of a sudden, unexpectantly, totally surprisingly, without warning me and especially NOT asking my permission

AAAOOOOOWWWWWWOOUUCCHHHHH!!!😲... Somebody bit my tail!!😥

I thought I was all alone in my egg, comfy, cozy, getting bigger and stronger and then it happened.

When I find out who bit me, I will bite them back really really hard....

but I can't see in here and when I called out "Who is there?"", no one answered....

the coward, that invisible chomper, I will remember this for when I get out of my shell.

We dragons never forget😠, not even a teeny, tiny, baby dragon🐲.

There is a mighty song I made up about frightening away this creepy bite maker,

so I sing it to myself when I go to sleep,

so I am ready if they ever try to bite me again.

I will be prepared!!!
